Frequency Asked Questions

What is Digital Waste?

Unnecessary data like duplicate photos and old emails that stay stored on servers — consuming energy.

Are these numbers accurate?

They are illustrative estimates based on average data center energy intensity.

Does deleting email really help?

Individually, it's small. But collectively, it stops data centers from needing to expand infrastructure unnecessarily.

Is digital waste “real” waste?

Yes. It’s not trash you can see, but the storage and network activity require real electricity, water for cooling, and raw materials for hardware.

How can I lower my footprint?

Delete old files, stream responsibly (don't autoplay while sleeping), and choose cloud providers powered by renewable energy.

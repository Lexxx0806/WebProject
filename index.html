Quick Actions

  • Use dark mode: Saves energy on OLED screens
  • Delete old emails: Gmail alone stores 1.5PB of data
  • Choose green hosting: Support eco-friendly providers
  • Reduce video quality: 480p uses 60% less data than 4K
  • Empty cloud storage: Unused files still consume energy
  • Support green websites: Every click matters

Future Technologies

Innovative solutions are emerging to make digital infrastructure inherently sustainable and efficient.

AI-Powered Optimization: Machine learning algorithms that automatically optimize code and reduce energy consumption by up to 40%.
Edge Computing: Processing data closer to users reduces long-distance data transmission and lowers global energy use.
Green Data Centers: Facilities powered by 100% renewable energy with advanced cooling systems using 50% less electricity.
Carbon-Aware Computing: Systems that automatically shift workloads to regions with cleaner energy sources.

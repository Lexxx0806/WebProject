Mission Report

Technical analysis of the "Lite" initiative.

1. The Problem

Digital activity is often perceived as carbon-neutral. However, the infrastructure supporting the internet accounts for 3.7% of global greenhouse emissions—comparable to the aviation industry. "Dark Data" (unused files) creates a phantom load on this infrastructure.

2. The Methodology

Our calculations utilize the Sustainable Web Design model:

  • Storage: Average of 0.2kg CO2e per GB per year (Source: Chia Network / IEA).
  • Streaming: 0.055kg CO2e per hour of HD video (Source: Carbon Trust).
  • Email: 0.3g (spam) to 50g (attachment) per email (Source: Berners-Lee).

3. The Solution

Lite proposes a "Behavior-First" approach. By gamifying the deletion of data, we aim to reduce server load at the source—the user.
